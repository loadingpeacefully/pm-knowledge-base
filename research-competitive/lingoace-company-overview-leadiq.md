# LingoAce Company Overview – LeadIQ Profile

## Source
LeadIQ B2B intelligence page for LingoAce — company profile, headcount, funding, and competitive landscape data.

## Company Profile

### About LingoAce
- **Type:** Online K-12 EdTech platform
- **Headquarters:** Singapore
- **Focus:** Chinese language learning, with expansion into Math and Music
- **Target Market:** Chinese diaspora families globally; non-Chinese families learning Mandarin

### Business Scale (as of LeadIQ data)
- Raised significant venture capital (Series A/B from investors including GIC, Owl Ventures, Peak XV Partners)
- Headcount in the hundreds with significant Singapore/China engineering presence
- Serves students across North America, Southeast Asia, and other Chinese diaspora markets

### Competitive Landscape (per LeadIQ)
- Competitors include: VIPKid, iTalki, GoGoKid, Duolingo, BrightCHAMPS (EdTech adjacent)
- Differentiation: premium small-group Chinese language instruction with qualified teachers

## Key Intelligence Points

### Funding & Growth
- Backed by Peak XV Partners (formerly Sequoia India/SEA) — signals strong investor confidence in the premium EdTech segment
- Significant investment in curriculum development and teacher network

### Product Strategy
- Expanded from Chinese language → Math → Music (multi-subject platform strategy)
- Focus on "Award-Winning" branding suggests heavy investment in brand credibility

### Go-To-Market
- Parent-facing marketing heavily emphasizes teacher quality and small class sizes
- Demo-to-paid conversion is a key funnel metric (similar to BrightCHAMPS)

## Relevance for Competitive Analysis

**Similarities to BrightCHAMPS:**
- Live online classes with trained teachers
- Demo → Paid conversion funnel
- Multi-subject expansion strategy
- Global diaspora and premium market focus

**Key Differentiators:**
- LingoAce is language-focused at core; BrightCHAMPS is coding/STEM-first
- LingoAce's 1v4 model vs. BrightCHAMPS' 1:1 and 1:20 models
- BrightCHAMPS has a stronger gamification and content automation story
