# Teach Online with LingoAce 2025 – ESL Teacher 365

## Source
ESL Teacher 365 article reviewing what it's like to teach with LingoAce — an independent third-party resource for online English and language teachers evaluating platforms.

## Key Content

### LingoAce Teaching Overview (2025 Edition)
- **Subject coverage:** Chinese (Mandarin), Math, and Music
- **Class format:** 1v4 small group model
- **Teacher requirements:** Native/near-native Chinese or English speaker; teaching certification preferred
- **Pay rates:** Typically stated in $/hour for ESL-adjacent platforms (range varies by experience and class type)
- **Scheduling:** Teacher sets availability; platform allocates students

### Platform Experience for Teachers
- **Onboarding:** Multi-step teacher certification process including demo class evaluation
- **Materials:** Curriculum materials provided by LingoAce (teachers follow structured lesson plans)
- **Tech platform:** Proprietary classroom environment with virtual whiteboard and interactive tools
- **Support:** Dedicated teacher support team (responsiveness varies per community reports)

### Pros Cited by ESL Teacher 365
- Competitive pay for qualified teachers
- Structured curriculum reduces lesson planning burden
- Small class size (1v4) allows better student-teacher relationships
- Growing platform with consistent student demand

### Cons Cited
- Strict curriculum adherence leaves little room for teacher creativity
- Class allocation can be inconsistent (especially for newer teachers)
- Platform technical issues during peak hours
- Requires investment in teaching setup (stable internet, good camera, quiet space)
