# LingoAce Revolutionizes Math Learning with Major AceMath Upgrade for Global Students

## Source
Press release / news article announcing LingoAce's AceMath product upgrade — a major enhancement to their math learning platform targeting global student audience.

## Key Announcements

### AceMath Platform Upgrade
- "AceMath" is LingoAce's branded math product — this upgrade is a significant product milestone
- "Revolutionizes" and "Major Upgrade" language signals this is a flagship investment in the math vertical
- Targeting global students — not just the Chinese diaspora market

### Likely Upgrade Components
- **Curriculum:** Expanded skill coverage, alignment with international standards (Common Core, Singapore Math)
- **Interactive features:** New gamified or interactive lesson formats
- **Assessment:** Enhanced diagnostic and formative assessment tools
- **Teacher experience:** Improved teaching aids and classroom tools
- **Progress tracking:** Better student progress visualization for parents

### Strategic Context
- This upgrade comes after LingoAce's initial Math expansion (adding math to its Chinese-only platform)
- Signals that Math is now a priority investment area — not just an add-on
- "Global students" framing indicates ambition beyond Chinese diaspora families

## Competitive Intelligence

### Direct Competition with BrightCHAMPS Math Vertical

Both LingoAce AceMath and BrightCHAMPS' Math vertical (launched JAS '24 – OND '24) are competing in the same segment:

| Dimension | LingoAce AceMath | BrightCHAMPS Math |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Launch approach | Upgrade to existing platform | 0→1 new vertical build |
| Curriculum alignment | Likely Singapore Math | US Common Core |
| Class format | 1v4 small group | 1:1 and 1:20 group |
| Content generation | Manual/traditional | AI-powered (Geeta-AI) |
| Interactive learning | Platform-native features | JSON-driven 40+ template library |

### Key Insight
- LingoAce's credibility in structured curriculum (Singapore Math heritage) is their moat in the math space
- BrightCHAMPS' competitive advantage is content production velocity (AI pipeline), gamification depth, and lower cost per student via group classes
