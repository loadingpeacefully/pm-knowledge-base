## Key Projects and Outcomes by Quarter

### AMJ '24 (April–June 2024)

**[P1] Platform Enablement for Offline Summer Camp Delivery**
- Enabled offline-compatible version of the gamified platform for Summer Camp 2024 with a reward-led learning experience and lesson progress syncing.
- Implemented UI flows in the absence of a designer; coordinated with ops, tech, and content teams.
- **Outcome:** 47% of enrolled students (80/172) completed at least one lesson independently. 6 students converted to Master Course enrollments.

**[P2] Gamified Assessment Design & Adoption**
- Drove end-to-end development of Quiz Galaxy for Financial Literacy (AI), integrating revision cards, timed assessments, and instant feedback.
- Co-created quiz modules with teachers, integrating feedback to align flow with class delivery.
- **Outcome:** 89% quiz completion across unlocked quizzes — highest across all courses, nearly 2× the next best. 70%+ students scored ≥80%.

**[P3] Report a Bug System**
- Led the conception and rollout of a real-time bug reporting system for interactive learning modules.
- Enabled structured tagging and backend routing to tech/content/design teams.
- **Outcome:** Iterated feature based on usage; directly contributed to improved module ratings (4.5+ across FinLit modules).

**[P4] Math Curriculum System Design**
- Defined the global math curriculum structure via deep-dive benchmarking (US + competitors: Kumon, Koobits, SplashLearn).
- **Outcome:** 4-layered skill-based framework enabling content reuse and geographic localization.
