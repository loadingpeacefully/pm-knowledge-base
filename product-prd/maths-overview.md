# Maths Overview

## Overview

High-level overview of the BrightCHAMPS Mathematics vertical — scope, curriculum framework, product components, and strategic positioning in the K-12 online math education market.

## Strategic Context

### Why BrightCHAMPS Launched Math
- Core verticals (Coding, Robotics, FinLit) served a specific niche; Math is a universal TAM expansion
- Math is the highest-demand K-12 tutoring subject globally — Kumon, IXL, SplashLearn dominate
- BrightCHAMPS' existing teacher network and platform infrastructure could support math delivery
- Group class model (1:20) makes Math economically viable at a lower price point than 1:1

### Target Market
- **Primary:** K–8 students in US/UK/ANZ/SEA markets
- **Secondary:** India, Vietnam (high parental investment in academic math tutoring)
- **Positioning:** "Interactive math, not just drill" — differentiated from Kumon (rote repetition) and IXL (static practice)

## Curriculum Architecture

### 4-Layered Skill Framework

```
Milestone (e.g., "Operations with Fractions")
  └── Skill (e.g., "Add fractions with unlike denominators")
        └── Sub-skill (e.g., "Find LCM of two numbers")
              └── Activity (individual gamified tasks)
```

- Curriculum aligned to **US Common Core** as the primary standard
- International alignment available for other markets (Singapore Math methodology informed design)
- Sub-skills mapped to difficulty levels: Foundational → Grade-Level → Advanced

### Curriculum Coverage
- **Grade bands:** Grades 1–8 (K covered via demo content)
- **Topic areas:** Number Operations, Fractions & Decimals, Algebra Foundations, Geometry, Statistics
- **Lesson count target:** 2,000+ interactive lessons (enabled by AI Content Factory)

## Product Components

### 1. Live Class Experience
- 45–60 minute live sessions (teacher + student/group)
- Lesson flow: Recap → Spotlight → Guided Practice
- Templates: Math-specific activities (equation input, fraction widgets, geometry hotspots)

### 2. Numon: Practice Zone (Self-Paced)
- Kumon-style daily practice product
- Worksheet-based progression with milestone gates
- AI-generated question variations to prevent rote memorization
- Retry logic + gamified summaries (XP, diamonds, achievements)

### 3. AI Content Factory (Geeta-AI)
- Automated worksheet generation from curriculum metadata
- 15x scale: 10 worksheets/week → 150 worksheets/week
- Validation middleware: 0% syntax error rate in production

## Teacher Requirements for Math
- Math-qualified teachers with subject matter competency
- Training on BrightCHAMPS Math curriculum delivery standards
- DQS framework applies (same as other verticals)

## Competitive Differentiation

| Feature | Kumon | IXL | BrightCHAMPS Math |
|---------|-------|-----|-------------------|
| Delivery | Worksheet drills | Online practice | Live class + AI practice |
| Interaction | Independent | Self-paced | Teacher-facilitated + gamified |
| Feedback | Delayed (weekly) | Immediate | Real-time (in-class + post-class) |
| Cost | Premium | Subscription | Premium live + subscription practice |
| Personalization | Pace-adaptive | Adaptive practice | AI-generated variations + teacher adaptation |

## Key Metrics (Target)
- Demo-to-paid conversion: ≥15% (matching Coding/FinLit benchmarks)
- Group class adoption: 65%+ teacher adoption (achieved in Phase 1)
- Student retention: <5% monthly churn
- Practice completion rate: ≥70% of assigned worksheets completed
