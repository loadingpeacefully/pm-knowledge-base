# Architectural Innovation to Product Management Excellence

## Overview

Synthesized career narrative spanning Suneet Jagdev's professional journey from computational architecture to EdTech product management. Anchored by a B.Arch from IIT Kharagpur, the trajectory demonstrates rapid evolution across Construction, Logistics, and EdTech sectors.

## 3. BrightCHAMPS: Scale, Migration, and Gamification (2023–2024)

**Focus:** Managed diverse teams (3 to 40 engineers); unified fragmented verticals

### A. The Unified Migration (The Backbone Project)
- BrightCHAMPS had fragmented dashboards for Coding, Robotics, and Financial Literacy
- Achieved **100% user migration** for students and teachers across key verticals
- Built login module, user profile, class joining flows, and certificate generation from scratch
- **Impact:** Centralized operations for US/Europe expansion

### B. Gamified Learning (Project X & FinLit)
- Introduced Badges (Skill Surge, Rapid Ranger), XP systems, and Leaderboards
- Quiz Galaxy: increased quiz completion from **40% to 89%**
- FinLit conversion improved **100% (8% → 16.5%)** within a single quarter in MEA region

### C. The Nano Skills Ecosystem (Product-Led Growth)
- Identified "Diamond Hoarding" problem — students had currency but nowhere to spend it
- Built Nano Skills marketplace: self-paced micro-courses unlockable via diamonds
- Introduced "Smart Modals" to recover lost conversions
- Led multilingual support initiative (Vietnamese) for non-English markets

### D. AI & Operational Excellence
- AI-powered worksheet generator scaled content production: **10 → 150 worksheets/week**
- Reduced operational costs by **90%**
- Conceptualized TrialBuddy AI chatbot to reduce support dependency
