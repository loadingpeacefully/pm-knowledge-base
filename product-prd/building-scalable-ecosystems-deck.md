## Section 1: BrightCHAMPS (Feb 2023 – Present)

### Slide 3: The "Unified Migration" (The Backbone Project)

**Context:** BrightCHAMPS had fragmented dashboards (Coding, Robotics, FinLit)
**The Problem:** Disjointed experience; impossible to cross-sell or track holistic progress
**The Solution:** Migrated all verticals (Student, Teacher, Admin) to a single "Unified Dashboard"

**Key Features Delivered:**
- **One Dashboard:** Centralized Login, Profile, and Class Scheduling
- **Calendar Platform:** Unified scheduling for Repeat (LTA) and One-time availability
- **Group 360:** Admin functionality for group classes and cohorts

**Impact:** 100% user migration achieved; centralized operations for US/Europe expansion

### Slide 4: Gamification & Retention (Project X)

**The Solution:**
- **The Feed:** "Global Feed" for student projects + "Personal Feed" for class updates
- **Badges & Leaderboards:** "Rapid Ranger" and "Skill Surge" badges for attendance/consistency
- **Quiz Galaxy:** Gamified post-class assessments to replace boring homework

**Impact:** Quiz completion: 40% → 90%; 70% of students scoring >80%

### Slide 5: Nano Skills & The Diamond Economy (PLG)

**The Problem:** "Diamond Hoarding" — students accumulated currency but had nowhere to spend it; high dependency on sales agents for renewal

**The Solution:**
- **Nano Skills Marketplace:** Self-paced courses (YouTuber, GenAI) unlockable via diamonds
- **Harvard Integration:** Certified Harvard courses within dashboard
- **Localization:** Multilingual support (Vietnamese) for non-English markets

**Impact:** 3,000+ enrollments; 5% gross margin uplift in Year 1

### Slide 6: AI & Operational Efficiency

**The Solution:**
- **AI Worksheet Generator:** "Geeta-AI" auto-generates math questions from curriculum metadata
- **TrialBuddy:** AI chatbot for demos handling "Where is my link?" queries, reducing support tickets
- **Teacher Prioritization:** Ranks teachers by conversion history (US/ROW/Vietnam specific)

**Impact:** Content production: 10 → 150 worksheets/week; Ops cost reduced by 90%
