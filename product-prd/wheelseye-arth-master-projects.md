---

## WHEELSEYE (Logistics Tech)

**Role:** Associate Product Manager (Apr 2021 – Feb 2022)
**Core Thesis:** Transform a manual, opaque logistics brokerage into a real-time, trust-based digital marketplace.

### PILLAR 1: Marketplace Dynamics & Liquidity

#### 1. Real-Time Truck Booking Marketplace
- **Tag:** Marketplace Efficiency / Algorithmic Matching
- **Problem:** Manual phone-based negotiations; consignors waited hours for truck confirmation
- **Solution:**
  - Moved from manual assignment to algorithmic allocation (operator proximity + route history)
  - Integrated "OpSearch" to auto-fetch quotes from nearby operators within 60-minute window
- **Impact:** Truck confirmation: **8 hours → 5 minutes**

#### 2. The "Price Visibility" Experiment
- **Tag:** User Psychology / Transparency / Trust
- **Problem:** Consignors dropped off during "Searching" phase — no visibility into quote status
- **Solution:**
  - Showed first 5 quotes received in real-time during first 30 minutes (vs. hiding until final price)
  - Psychological status timers: "Validating documents...", "First price found", "Expanding search"
  - Countdown urgency: "Hurry! Choose best price now" sticky header when quotes available

### PILLAR 2: Trust & Operational Digitization

#### 3. Digital Consignor Onboarding (KYC)
- **Tag:** User Acquisition / Compliance
- **Problem:** Paper-heavy, physical document verification process
- **Impact:** Onboarding time: **1 week → 10 minutes**

#### 4. Real-Time Billing ("Digital Bilti")
- **Tag:** FinTech / Operations
- **Problem:** Delays in invoice (Bilti) generation, low payment transparency
- **Solution:** Instant digital invoice generated upon booking confirmation
- **Impact:** Improved payment transparency and finance ops efficiency

## ARTH DESIGN BUILD (Construction Tech)

**Role:** Product Manager / Sr. Consultant (2020–2023, 2024–Present)
**Core Thesis:** Bringing Computational Design and SaaS Automation to the traditional construction industry.

### PILLAR 1: B2B SaaS (0→1 Launch)

#### 1. BuildwIT (Feasibility Tool)
- **Tag:** 0→1 Product Launch / B2B SaaS
- **Problem:** Architects spent weeks manually calculating feasibility for new real estate projects
- **Solution:**
  - Browser-based feasibility tool: input plot parameters → instant feasibility reports
  - A/B tested Sliders vs. Manual input to optimize UX for non-tech-savvy architects
- **Impact:** **10 paying clients within 2 months** of MVP release

### PILLAR 2: Computational Design & Automation

#### 2. Internal Workflow Automation (The Efficiency Engine)
- **Tag:** Operational Efficiency / Cost Reduction
- **Problem:** Manual design iterations for large infrastructure projects were error-prone and slow
- **Solution:** Visual scripting to automate repetitive design tasks (drawing generation, quantity calculation)
- **Impact:** Process time reduced by **95%**; saved **1,000+ man-hours** and **₹73.6 Lakh in one month**

#### 3. Advanced Simulation (GMR Airport & Godrej)
- **Tag:** Technical Innovation / Client Success
- **GMR Airport:** 4D VR simulations for terminal construction sequencing; improved stakeholder alignment
- **Godrej:** Parametric analysis of "Daylight and Views" for residential complexes; optimized building orientation

### PILLAR 3: Strategy & Enablement

#### 4. BIM/VDC Practice Strategy
- **Tag:** Go-to-Market Strategy
- Defined BIM service offerings, pricing models, and GTM strategy for enterprise clients
- Drove adoption of VR and Computational Design through internal pilots and training
- Enabled team transition from manual CAD to digital workflows
